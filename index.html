From clay to creation

Claya is a space where creativity meets clay. Our studio was created to share the art of pottery with everyone. Whether you’re a curious beginner or an experienced maker, our workshops invite you to explore, learn, and enjoy the timeless craft of pottery in an inspiring and welcoming atmosphere.

Before you get your hands dirty

All tools and clay are provided. We recommend wearing comfortable clothes that can get messy, and bringing an apron if you like.

We have classes for different levels. Beginners class is suitable for you if it’s your very first time working with clay. We’ll go through all the basics step by step.

Your creations will be glazed and fired in our studio. Once they are ready, we’ll let you know and you can come pick them up.

The whole process, including drying, glazing, and firing, usually takes 2–3 weeks. We’ll notify you once your work is ready.
